About

I collaborate with a team of musicians to provide live music for all kinds of occasions. Whether it is a wedding, festival, bhajan, or baby shower, I can create a custom plan to make your event special.

Weddings

I specialize in live music for Gujarati weddings with traditional Lagna Geet throughout the ceremony. The songs create an uplifting atmosphere from the moment the groom enters the hall to the end of the vidaai ceremony. I also do Bollywood music for mehndi and Sangeet nights. I bring my own sound system and speaker set-up to ensure the best quality.

Details

Pricing depends on many factors, such as the location of the event, number of musicians needed, and the size of the event. For a free quote, please contact me.
