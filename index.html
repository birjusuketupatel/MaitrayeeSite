About Me

My name is Maitrayee Patel. Since 2006, I have dedicated myself to the preservation of Hindustani classical music by teaching students of all ages.

During my lessons, I help students connect with their roots. I want to inspire my students to embrace Indian culture and be proud of their heritage. I hope to pass on the knowledge I accumulated from my guru in India to the next generation in the here in the US.

I am based in West Chester, Pennsylvania, but I also offer online classes, which are open to anyone regardless of location.

I also provide live music for weddings, raas garba, and other events. I have performed across the nation, and I work with other experienced artists to make sure the music for your event is customized to your needs.
