About My School

Since 2006, I have taught Indian classical music to students of all ages and skill levels. My curriculum is based on Hindustani classical singing as it is traditionally taught in India. Other genres, such as religious songs, folk songs, and Bollywood music, are also introduced to students.
Lessons are 1 hour long and are conducted once per week.

Group Lessons

In my group lessons, beginners are paired with peers of their same skill level, and work through the curriculum together. Each group contains between 5 to 10 students. Classes are conducted online only.
Tuition is $120 per month.

Private Lessons

In my private lessons, a group of 1 to 3 students are given personalized, one-on-one instruction. This curriculum is for advanced students with a passion for music. Classes are conducted online.
Tuition is $160 per month.
Due to high demand, availability is limited.
